Instances Trevoh uses Humor

Trevoh has used a lot of humor in his book, some of the instances are when his mother throws him out of a moving vehicle at the beginning of the book to save themselves but then Trevoh instead of being grateful for that he questions his mother asking why she threw him out ”At least they would have woken me up before they killed me” this shows even as a small child Trevoh could find a way to turn any situation into some form of a joke.
Another instance is when Trevoh and the mother were not talking to each other she opted to send him letters to pass her points and Trevoh wouldn’t have to argue with her, but Trevoh still sent her replies challenging her like the instance where the mother was talking about his grades and he still got to tell the mum that he got it from her.
When Trevor was about to get mugged by a group of Zulu guys because they thought he was white but Trevor used his humor to evade been mugged and told them that instead of him they should all mug someone else and by this, he was able to evade been mugged.
Trevoh never fitted in any group be it the blacks he was told that he was too light and with the whites he was told that he was too dark again in this sence Trevoh used his sence of humor to atleast get friends like in school he never really had a group of people he could call his friend but he was able to hang out with the "cool kids in school". Trevoh was known to be a naughty kid even when he was a little boy for instance at school when he got punished by the principal he laughed I mean in normal settings when a kid is been spanked or beaten they are expected to be crying ,but Trevoh instead used to laugh because he thought that the principal spankings were weak ,and the principal sent him to see a phychologist because that is not normal for any child.
